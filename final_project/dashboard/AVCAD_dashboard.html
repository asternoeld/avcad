1 · Crops in environmental space (PCA)

Each point is one observation, projected onto the first two principal components. Hover for the crop; use the control to colour by group or isolate one crop.

PC1 is a soil-fertility axis (phosphorus & potassium); PC2 a moisture/temperature axis (humidity). Loading arrows shown in grey.

2 · Environmental fingerprint of each crop

Standardized mean requirement (z-score) of a chosen crop across the seven variables — its agro-environmental signature.

Left: national yields 2015–2025 on the world map. Right: world-average yield trend since 1990. Choose a crop.
